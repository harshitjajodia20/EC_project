Grocery Delivery in Pandemic

Amid this unfortunate pandemic, people are forced to stay inside their house and maintain social distancing under the government norms. And in such times we have observed cases of scarcity of daily use groceries in various areas around the country as people have been stocking up the same to keep themselves at ease. We want you to create an app or a web app that can help the people who are facing the issues.

Overview

Firing Guns introduces a unique way to deliver groceries in pandemic.

Addition of Grocery in Access

You can add the groceries which you have in access and want to give it to others. You can decide the price, provide it for free or charge some amount. You can choose who you want to sell or do charity. Your identity remains safe with us.


Learn More

Demand Grocery

You can see the list of grocery which are currently present and people are willing to give it in your pincode. You can also add a request of grocery which you require. Once a seller is available, he/she can contact you and give you the necessary details regarding delivery of grocery.

Learn More

Nutrition Requirements

You can directly check your nutrition requirements from web app. This is something which people should think about if they have any defficiency. This is a add-on which we provide. People can directly check the necessary food which they need to keep them healthy and order from the available list.

Learn More

Support from Firing Guns

You can directly contact Firing Guns team regarding your queries and you will get support from the experts at Firing Guns. A message regarding bugs or suggestions can be sent by the user.
